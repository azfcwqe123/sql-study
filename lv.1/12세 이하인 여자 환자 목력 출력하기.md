---

Oracle

```SQL
SELECT PT_NAME, PT_NO, GEND_CD, AGE, NVL(TLNO, 'NONE')
FROM PATIENT
WHERE AGE <= 12 AND GEND_CD = 'W'
ORDER BY AGE DESC, PT_NAME ASC
```

---

MySQL

```SQL
SELECT PT_NAME, PT_NO, GEND_CD, AGE, IFNULL(TLNO, 'NONE')
FROM PATIENT
WHERE AGE <= 12 AND GEND_CD = 'W'
ORDER BY AGE DESC, PT_NAME ASC
```

---

### TIP

1. COALESCE는 Oracle이나 MySQL 모두 사용 가능.
