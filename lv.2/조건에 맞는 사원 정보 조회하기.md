MySQL

```SQL
SELECT
    SUM(B.SCORE) SCORE,
    B.EMP_NO,
    A.EMP_NAME,
    A.POSITION,
    A.EMAIL
FROM
    HR_EMPLOYEES A
INNER JOIN
    HR_GRADE B
    ON A.EMP_NO = B.EMP_NO
GROUP BY
    B.EMP_NO
ORDER BY 
    SCORE DESC
LIMIT 1
```
